About Munduk Village

Munduk is known as a beautiful mountain village with authentic nature, located in the central north of Bali islands. Munduk is situated between 500 - 1200 meters above sea level. This range gives Mundukâ€™s inhabitants the opportunity to plant various kinds of plants.

Only Tamblingan lake and surrounding jungles are conservation areas. The rest of it is farming land which is owned by around 2000 families or about 6000 inhabitants. Means, if you do trekking around Munduk, actually you walk on someone's private land. For this reason, a local guide is needed to keep in harmony with local inhabitants and to experience their culture and nature.
