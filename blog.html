Tour Package Waterfalls Trek

Introduction

As Munduk is the house of some of the best waterfalls in Bali, trekking to some waterfalls should be one of your list while visiting Munduk village. On this trek, we going to see three stunning waterfalls which are named RED CORAL, LABUHAN KEBO and LAANGAN waterfall.

Please note that the whole area along the paths to waterfalls are not wild forest but private farmers land as their plantation. For that reason the Journey to waterfalls is another valuable thing beside the waterfall itself. Our experience guide will be very enthusiastic to discuss the farming system and show you many different types of plants that are growing along the trek. The local guide will assist you to recognise some fruits, spices and herb medicine along the trek.
